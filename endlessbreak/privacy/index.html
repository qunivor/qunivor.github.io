Data Collection

EndlessBreak does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data in this release.

Local Gameplay Data

Gameplay progress and settings are saved only on the user's device using Unity PlayerPrefs. This local data is used to keep weapon progress, game settings, and similar gameplay state. Deleting the app removes this local data from the device.

Third-Party Services

This release does not use ads, analytics, in-app purchases, account login, leaderboard login, location, camera, microphone, contacts, notifications, or cloud services.

Future Updates

If a future update adds ads, leaderboard, analytics, account features, online services, or any third-party SDK that collects or transmits user data, this policy and the Google Play Data safety section must be updated before that version is released.

Data Retention and Deletion

EndlessBreak stores gameplay progress locally until the user deletes the app or clears the app data from the device. 큐니버(Qunivor) does not retain a server-side copy because no gameplay or personal data is transmitted to the developer in this release.
